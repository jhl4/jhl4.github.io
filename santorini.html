Online Santorini (inspired by the classic board game)

What is Santorini?

Santorini is a 2-player board game. The objective of the game is to move one of your workers to the third level. On a given turn, a player will move a worker to an adjacent space and then place a building with that worker on an adjacent space. There are 2 modes that you can play: with god cards and without god cards. God cards provide the player with a special ability.

See the full rules here.

About my implementation:

  • My backend is written in Java, and tested with Apache Maven. Tests have appropriate specification coverage.
  • My frontend was built using Typescript React and HTML/CSS.
  • I connected the frontend and the backend with NanoHTTPD to integrate HTTP server functionality.
  • I adhered to code-level design principles during the development of this game, such as cohesion, encapsulation, and minimizing coupling.
  • Containerized with Docker, and hosted on Google Cloud Compute.
